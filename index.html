What are Vegetarian Investments about?

Being a True Vegetarian

Vegetarianism is not just limited to food. No doubt that by following a vegetarian diet you save the life of an animal every day but by investing your money in businesses that promote non-animal products you save lives of millions of animals

Blood Money

Being vegetarian we do not kill animals ourselves but indirectly we can be part of such an exploitation. By investing in companies that use and benefit from animal derived products, we fund their businesses that leads to killing millions of animals every day!

Investments = Your Values

By following vegetarian investments, you can rest easily by knowing that your money is invested in line with your ethics. Your investments will no more lead to animal testing, farming, eating, hunting or racing.

Ethical Investments

By carefully choosing the businesses that you invest your money in, you do not contribute to the animal exploitation and suffering, including the destruction of natural habitats.

 When you make a financial investment, you are also making an investment in the type of future you want. This is at the heart of ethical investment. Do you want to invest in a world with more live animal exports, nuclear weapons and poker machines, or a world with more clean energy, healthy food and accessible education?

Ethical investing is about seeking a good financial return by investing in ways that align your money with your values. It means the investor accounts for the whole impact of a company, not just their bottom line. The benefits go beyond a clean conscience and a better society.
