Vegetarianism

Definition

According to the dictionary meaning of this word - "Vegetarianism is the practice of staying away from the consumption of meat (including poultry, seafood, and the flesh of any other animal)". We apply this to only food. We assume that by living on a diet of grains, vegetables, fruits and other non-animal-based foods we are vegetarian but this is not the case. 

By not eating animal products we are saving a life of an animal but by investing our money in businesses that cause cruelty and death of animals we are killing millions of animals 

Vegetarian Lifestyle
Live & let live

Not just food but adopting a lifestyle involving no or minimal cruelty to animals in aspects including food, attire (clothing, footwear, belts, jewellery, watches) and accessaries (purses, wallets, bags etc), household goods (materials used in construction, decoration and maintenance of house), health and medicine, personal hygiene and beautification and livelihood (investments)

Financial Investments

For any business to run, it needs a supply of money from investors. It can be said that businesses rely on people investing and giving them money to run the business in return for a monetary share in the profits. Thus investors are doubly associated with the business - they make it possible and they reap the rewards. 

Investors in the meat industries are willingly and knowingly providing financial support for the killing of innocent animals.

It is ironical that many investors in the meat industries are vegetarians themselves
