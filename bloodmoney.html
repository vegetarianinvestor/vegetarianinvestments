How do investors think?

Best investment = Business that can make good profit.

When chosing a company we generally look at balance sheet, profit and loss (P&L) account and cash flow statements.  

No doubt that these figures should be looked at but these should be secondary. As vegetarians our primary criteria should be understanding how the company is making money. If its by killing animals then we shouldn't be giving our money to these companies to grow their business. It is like not going to shops that offer meat even if they give are cheap.

Mutuals Funds

Now a days most of the investments are done in mutual funds. Based on the type of mutual fund the investments are done but the general formula is invest in any business that brings profit.

These businesses could be companies that breed and kill animals, companies that produce products containing animal ingredients (for e.g. leather, paints, jewellary etc) or companies that are suppliers of raw materials to other companies.
